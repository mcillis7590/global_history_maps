Welcome to Mr. Cillis' Global History Maps Site

Welcome to our class website focusing on geography. Throughout history, humans have had to adapt and interact with their geography. The customs, cultures, and ways of life of civilizations are often influenced by the geography around them. The purpose of this website, is to help you visualize the impact that geography has had on humans throughout the time periods covered in our class.
